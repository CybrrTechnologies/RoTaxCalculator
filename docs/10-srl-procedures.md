## 1. Prelungire Sediu Social (Headquarters Lease Extension)

When your SRL's rental or comodatum (free use) contract for the registered headquarters expires, you must extend it with ONRC to avoid penalties.

### 1.1 When to File

**Timeline:**
- File starting from the **first day** the new contract takes effect
- **Submission Deadline**: No later than **30 days** from when the new contract's validity begins
- **Grace Period**: Additional **30 days** from the old contract's expiration before ANAF declares company inactive
- If you receive a notification from Trade Register, you have **30 days** to respond

**Two Separate 30-Day Periods:**

1. **Submission Window (After New Contract Starts)**
   - Starts: First day new lease/comodatum takes effect
   - Ends: 30 days from new contract start date
   - Example: New contract starts Jan 1 ‚Üí File by Jan 30

2. **Grace Period (After Old Contract Expires)**
   - Starts: Day old contract expires
   - Ends: 30 days after expiration
   - Risk: After this, ANAF can declare company fiscally inactive
   - Example: Old contract expires Jan 1 ‚Üí Grace period ends Jan 30

> ‚ö†Ô∏è **Best Practice**: File BEFORE the old contract expires! Don't rely on the grace period. Submit 1-2 weeks before expiration to avoid any risk of inactive status.

### 1.2 Required Documents

#### For Main Headquarters (Sediu Social Principal)

1. **Cerere de depunere men»õiuni** (Request form for filing mentions)
   - Available at Trade Register office or online portal
   - Original required

2. **Act adi»õional** (Addendum to original contract)
   - Extends the validity period of the lease/comodatum contract
   - Must reference the original contract that established the headquarters
   - Must be signed by both parties (landlord + SRL representative)

3. **Proof of headquarters validity** (if applicable)
   - Property deed (if owned)
   - Lease contract (contract de √Ænchiriere)
   - Sublease contract
   - Comodatum contract (contract de comodat - free use)

#### For Secondary Headquarters / Punct de Lucru

- Amendment to articles of incorporation (statut) - **NOT required**

2. **Non-renewable comodatum**: If landlord refuses to renew, you must relocate headquarters

### 1.4 Fees

- **ONRC fee**: **FREE** (no charge for this mention)
- **Exception**: If ONRC needs to mail documents to a different county, mailing fees apply

### 1.5 Processing Time

**ONRC Official Timeline:**
- **Registrar decision**: **1 working day** (by law - "registratorul se pronun»õƒÉ √Æn termen de o zi lucrƒÉtoare")
- **Certificate issuance**: **~3 working days** from registration date (in practice)

**What this means:**

- The full certificate processing and issuance takes approximately 3 working days
- If there are issues, you'll be notified via portal/email to provide additional documents

### 1.6 How to Submit

#### Option A: In-Person at Trade Register (ONRC)

1. Prepare all documents (see Required Documents above)
2. Bind documents in a dossier
3. Submit to **local Trade Register office** (Office of Commerce Registry)
4. Receive registration confirmation
5. Wait ~3 working days for processing
6. Receive mention certificate (certificat de √Ænregistrare men»õiune)

3. Complete online form for "Prelungire sediu social"
4. Upload scanned documents (PDF format):
   - Act adi»õional (addendum)
   - Administrator ID
5. Pay online (if applicable)
6. Receive electronic confirmation
7. Download certificate when ready (~3 working days)

> üí° **Tip**: If you don't have a digital certificate, use Option A or hire a consultant (dianex.ro, startco.ro, etc.)

### 1.7 Consequences of Not Extending

If you don't file the extension before the contract expires:

1. **ANAF declares company fiscally inactive** (inactivƒÉ fiscal)
   - Cannot issue invoices
   - Tax authorities may initiate control procedures
   - Company appears as "inactive" in public records

2. **Administrator receives fiscal infraction** (contraven»õie fiscalƒÉ)
   - Recorded in administrator's personal file
   - **Blocks future roles**: Cannot be associate or administrator in other companies until cleared
   - Fine may be imposed

3. **Risk of ex officio dissolution** (dizolvare din oficiu)
   - Trade Register can dissolve company for non-compliance
   - Requires court process to reinstate

### 2.5 Troubleshooting Common Issues

- **Cause**: Certificate expired or not from accredited provider

- **Cause**: Forgot to sign PDF before upload
- **Solution**: Download document, sign it, re-upload

#### "Eroare la √ÆncƒÉrcarea documentului"

- **Cause**: Driver not installed for USB token

## 3. Other SRL Procedures

### 3.1 Schimbarea Sediului Social (Changing Headquarters)

**When needed:**
- Moving to a new address in the same county
- Moving to a different county
- Landlord refuses lease extension

**Required:**
- AGA decision (hotƒÉr√¢re asocia»õi)
- New lease/comodatum contract
- Updated articles of incorporation
- Official Gazette publication
- ONRC fee: varies by county

**More info**: [ONRC - Schimbarea sediului social](https://www.onrc.ro/index.php/ro/mentiuni/persoane-juridice/schimbarea-sediului-social-in-acelasi-judet)

### 3.2 Prelungirea Duratei de Func»õionare (Extending Operating Duration)

**When needed:**
- Company's operating period (from articles) is expiring
- Default: unlimited duration, but some SRLs have fixed terms

**Required (Two-stage process):**

**Stage I - AGA Decision:**
1. Registration request form
2. AGA resolution to extend duration
3. Proof of authorization
4. Official Gazette publication fee

**Stage II - Statute Amendment (after 30 days):**
1. Registration request form
2. Amended articles extending duration
3. Updated articles of incorporation
4. Official Gazette publication fee

**Processing**: 1 working day after Stage II submission

**More info**: [ONRC - Prelungirea duratei de func»õionare](https://www.onrc.ro/index.php/ro/mentiuni/persoane-juridice/prelungirea-duratei-de-functionare)

### 3.3 Majorarea Capitalului Social (Capital Increase)

**When needed:**
- Adding new shareholders
- Increasing company equity
- Required by law (certain activities require minimum capital)

**Required:**
- AGA decision
- Payment proof (cash contributions) or valuation report (in-kind)
- Updated articles of incorporation
- Official Gazette publication

### 3.4 Modificare Administrator (Changing Administrator)

**When needed:**
- Current administrator resigns
- Shareholders appoint new administrator

**Required:**
- AGA decision
- New administrator's acceptance and ID
- Proof new administrator has no fiscal infractions
- Updated articles of incorporation
- Official Gazette publication
